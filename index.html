About Our College

A Tradition of Academic Excellence

Established in 1949, Sripat Singh College has grown into a premier institution of higher learning in the district. Named after the benevolent Sripat Singh, the college is dedicated to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds.

We strive to create an environment where students can realize their potential and contribute meaningfully to society. Our sprawling campus, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and dedicated faculty ensure a holistic learning experience.

Mission

To impart value-based education and foster critical thinking.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in higher education and research.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
